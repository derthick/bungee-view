About Bungee View

Bungee View lets you search, browse, and data-mine an image collection. Bungee View is under active development.  I will appreciate any comments you send to

Mark Derthick
Human Computer Interaction Institute
Carnegie Mellon University

 

Thanks to Peter Brusilovsky, Memory Grober, Ben Shneiderman, John Zimmerman, and students in CMU's Fall 2004 graduate design studio for help with the design.

Bungee View is implemented in Piccolo, written by Ben Bederson at the University of Maryland HCIL.

Bungee View has been supported by the Advanced Research and Development Activity (ARDA) under contract number H98230-04-C-0406 and NBCHC040037. and by DARPA STTR contract DAAH01-03-C-R171, subcontracted from MayaViz, Ltd. Bob Futernick of the Fine Arts Museums of San Francisco provided images and text descriptions; Marti Hearst provided meta-data created by the Flamenco project, and gave helpful feedback on a previous iteration.
